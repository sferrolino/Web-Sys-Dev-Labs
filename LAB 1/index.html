Akatsuki Members Ranked

Sarah Ferrolino (500768674)

The Akatsuki is a group of rogue shinobi found in the anime Naruto Shippuden that act as antagonists against our heroes.

All of them played an essential role to the Shippuden series with their individual powers and backstories and so I ranked them accordingly, based on my own criteria.

  1. Itachi - Great character development 10/10.
  2. Pain - Dramatic arc, defeated by talk no jutsu 9/10 angsty teen.
  3. Deidara - I like his hair.
  4. Tobi - Too dramatic, but background story very interesting.
  5. Sasori - defeated by Sakura...loses cool points.
  6. Konan - Cute oragami gal.
  7. Kisame - baby shark dudududududud.
  8. Black and White Zetsu - chomper.
  9. Kakuzu - no comments, don't like his vibe.
  10. Hidan - killed Asuma and will never forgive him for that 0/10 kill him again.
